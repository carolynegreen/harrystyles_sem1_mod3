Dunkirk

In 2017, Harry Styles made his acting debut in Christopher Nolan’s war film, Dunkirk, a true story about stranded Allied soldiers on the shores of Dunkirk who were rescued by civilian boats. Even playing a minor character, Alex, audiences were stunned by Styles’ excellent performance.

I, myself, was completely overwhelmed by the experience and have now seen the film a number of times, just for his cameos. The film contained a star studded cast including Tom Hardy, Cillian Murphy, and Mark Rylance, but their performances were definitely overshadowed by Styles.

The film won numerous awards, mainly for film and sound editing, and was nominated for Best Director and Best Picture at the 90th Academy Awards in 2018. I, myself, would have awarded Harry Styles Best Actor for his performance, and the film Best Picture just because he was so magnificent in the film.
